Our Club

Bel Air Youth Club

Bel Air Youth Club is a non-profit organization, founded in 2023 to promote soccer ("The Beautiful Game") in Bel Air Riviere Seche.

Our Club provides young players with a fun, safe, enriched experience that teaches the core fundamental techniques and rich understanding of the game, improves fitness, develops leadership and team building skills, and prepares them for competitive team play.

We also gives youngsters a sense of belonging which helps them make new friends and builds their social circle outside school.

We also help them to develop into responsible citizens and educate them to stay away from illicit substances.
